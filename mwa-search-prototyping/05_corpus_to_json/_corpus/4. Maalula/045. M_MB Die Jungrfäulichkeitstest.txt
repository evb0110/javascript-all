001. ōṯ šappa, batte yixṭub eḥḏa atamōy.
002. zelle ʕal-ōḏ, mamrille rezla, w hōr rezla.
003. ōxer mett ahtunne ʕal_eḥḏa, ōmar meʕla atamōy.
004. xaṭpa w kallel aʕla.
005. awwal yōma ōmar batte yġarrbenna, yiḥəm ʕa minžat atamōy.
006. zelle mayṯēl zabərṯe w ḏahenla lawna ikkum, lawna iṣfur, lawna summuḳ.
007. hū w ʕapper leʕla, mʕaynya bē xann, minṣaṭla mamrōle: «yīīī, exma iḥmiṯ, bess ext hanna la iḥmiṯ!»
